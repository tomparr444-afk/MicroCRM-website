Intelligence Briefing

Everything you need to know about the Micro CRM platform.

Is the platform mobile friendly?
Absolutely. Micro CRM is built dynamically to re-flow perfectly on mobile devices. Your field engineers can view their weekly diary, update job statuses, and check dispatch routes directly from their phones.
Do I need my own API keys?
No. Micro CRM comes fully integrated with our enterprise API keys for both Google Maps routing and Gemini AI. You can start using all advanced features immediately upon deployment without needing to manage, configure, or pay for your own API developer accounts.
Can I bulk upload my existing clients?
Yes! Micro CRM features a robust Batch Ingestion tool. You can upload .xlsx files containing your current users or jobs, and the system will automatically process and geocode them onto the map.
Can I customize the map for my team?
Definitely. Micro CRM allows you to assign unique pin colors (like blue, green, red, purple, and orange) to specific engineers or job severities, giving your dispatchers a visual shorthand of the entire operation.
How secure is my operational data?
Your data is stored securely using Supabase (PostgreSQL), featuring enterprise-grade row-level security. Your customer lists, fleet data, and ticketing information are safely isolated to your specific Company ID.
