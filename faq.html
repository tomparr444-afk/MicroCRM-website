Intelligence Briefing

Everything you need to know about the KartaFlow platform.

Is the platform mobile friendly?
Absolutely. KartaFlow is built dynamically to re-flow perfectly on mobile devices. Your field engineers can view their weekly diary, update job statuses, and check dispatch routes directly from their phones.
Do I need my own API keys?
Yes. To utilize the advanced routing and AI features, you will need to input your own Google Maps API Key and Google Gemini API Key into the system settings.
Can I bulk upload my existing clients?
Yes! KartaFlow features a robust data upload tool. You can upload .xlsx files containing your current users or jobs, and the system will automatically process and geocode them onto the map.
Can I customize the map for my team?
Definitely. KartaFlow allows you to assign unique pin colors (like blue, green, red, purple, and orange) to specific engineers or job severities, giving your dispatchers a visual shorthand of the entire operation.
How secure is my operational data?
Your data is stored securely using Supabase (PostgreSQL), featuring enterprise-grade row-level security. Your customer lists, fleet data, and ticketing information are safely isolated to your specific Company ID.
